TO WEAR - HAUTE COUTURE


PROJECT

I CREATED THE LOGO AND THE BRAND IDENTITY FOR A CLOTHES BRAND NAMED “TO WEAR” WHICH TARGETS YOUNG ADULTS.


CHALLENGE

THE “TO WEAR” BRAND SHOULD REFLECT A NEW GENERATION AND ORIGINAL PUNK VALUES THAT ARE TODAY IN FULL EXPANSION AMONG SOME YOUNG PEOPLE. THIS IS A VERY INSPIRED STYLE. THE GOAL WAS TO PROUDLY EXHIBIT THE PUNK STYLE EVOLUTION JUST AS PLAYBOI CARTI DOES.


SOLUTION

BASED ON THE STYLE OF PLAYBOI CARTI AND WHAT HE BROUGHT TO THE FASHION INDUSTRY ESPECIALLY WITH GIVENCHY OR WHAT STÜSSY CREATES NOWADAYS, I IMAGINED A MINIMALIST AND HANDWRITTEN TEXT THAT I MADE WITH THAT CHIC PREMIUM SUBTITLE WHICH SAYS “HAUTE COUTURE” TO SHOW THE PREMIUM QUALITY OF THE BRAND.
ALL PHOTOS TAKEN BELONG TO ME. I TOOK THESE PICTURES WITH A FRIEND; THE GOAL HERE WAS TO REFLECT ON THE GIVENCHY VALUES OF TODAY WITH A REVISITED VINTAGE LOOK, OVEREXPOSED PHOTOS AND A DESIRED CONTRAST.




LEATHER, METAL, MESH, AND BLOOD-RED ARE ALL SARTORIAL TOOLS EMPLOYED BY THE ARTIST PLAYBOI CARTI ON A REGULAR BASIS, USING HIS WARDROBE TO HIGHLIGHT THE INFLUENCE THAT THESE SUBCULTURES HAVE HAD ON HIS MUSIC, HIS LIVE PERFORMANCES, AND HIS OVERALL OUTLOOK ON LIFE.
