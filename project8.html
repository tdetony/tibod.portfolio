PROJECT

BIOCYCLE IS A CARGO BIKE DELIVERY SERVICE FOR PEOPLE WHO CARE ABOUT ENVIRONMENTAL AND INNOVATIVE CONCEPTS.


CHALLENGE

THE VISUAL IDENTITY AND THE LOGO SHOULD SHOW ELEGANCE, MODERNITY, RAPIDITY, ECOLOGY, ENERGY AND GOOD QUALITY.


SOLUTION

I CREATED A MODERN LOGO WITH SPEED VALUES AND A THIN AND ELEGANT TYPOGRAPHY. I CHOOSE THE MINT GREEN WATER COLOR TO ALSO REPRESENT BOTH MODERNITY AND ENVIRONMENT.




THE BIOCYCLE LOGO IS MADE OF A BICYCLE WHEEL WHICH EVOKES ENVIRONMENTAL, SPEED & ELEGANT VALUES

COLOR : GREEN AS A MODERN SYMBOL.
SPEED TEXTURE : BICYCLE WHEEL, SPEED, ELEGANT.
CIRCLE : O IN “BIOCYCLE” , CONSISTENT.
TYPOGRAPHY : THIN, ELEGANT FOR THE PREMIUM ASPECT (GOOD QUALITY).
