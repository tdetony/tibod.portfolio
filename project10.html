ALDO CONTERNO - WINE LABELS



PROJECT

I DESIGNED A SERIE OF LABELS AND AN INFORMATIONAL BOOKLET FOR THE BRAND ALDO CONTERNO, BASED IN ITALY.


CHALLENGE

MY LABELS SHOULD BE DIFFERENT BUT CONNECTED TO THE BRAND IDENTITY AND THE TYPE OF WINE SHOULD MATCH WHAT ITALIANS WINES ALREADY DO.


SOLUTION

BASED ON THE STYLE OF ALDO CONTERNO, I REFLECTED THE NATURE OF THE VINEYARD BY CREATING A VERY MINIMALIST LABEL WITH A LOT OF SPACE. THE MOST IMPORTANT PART IS TO WORK WITH THE TYPOGRAPHIES AND HERE, I SHOULD HAVE SERIF TYPOGRAPHY AND ALSO MANUSCRIT FOR THE TITLE TO SHOW THE PREMIUM ASPECT AND THE GOOD QUALITY. I THEREFORE CHOSE A SANS-SERIF FONT FOR THE PARAGRAPH TEXT.
