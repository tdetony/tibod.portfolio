OSTRICH CLUB


PROJECT

OSTRICH CLUB IS A CLUB FOR INTROVERTS, FOR PEOPLE WHO ARE SCARED TO JOIN A CLUB / BAR WITHOUT KNOWING WHO WILL BE THERE AND WHAT WILL HAPPEN. IT ALLOWS PEOPLE TO KNOW WHO WILL BE AT A PARTY THROUGH A MOBILE APP. THE APPLICATION WILL PROPOSE CONCEPT EVENINGS: VENUES AND RESERVATIONS EXCLUSIVELY FOR WOMEN, 50/50 MEN / WOMEN EVENINGS, SAME AGED EVENINGS OR LGBTQ+ EVENINGS - ALL OF THEM ALSO VALID WITH THEMED MUSIC EVENINGS THAT CAN SOMETIMES BE VOTED BY PEOPLE ON THEIR APPLICATION (HIP HOP, JAZZ, ROCK, HOUSE MUSIC...)


CHALLENGE

THIS CLUB FOR INTROVERTS SHOULD SHOW A FRIENDLY AND OPEN-MINDED ASPECT AND KEEP PEOPLE COMFORTABLE WITHOUT BEING STRESSED BY ANY UNFORESEEN CIRCUMSTANCES.


SOLUTION

THE LOGO REPRESENTS AN OSTRICH STICKING ITS HEAD INTO THE GROUND: A METAPHOR FOR THE INTROVERTED PEOPLE WHO STAY AT HOME OR ARE THERE BUT KEEP A LOW PROFILE. THAT HANDMADE CUSTOM TYPEFACE CLEARLY SHOWS THIS FRIENDLY AND YOUTHFUL ASPECT OF OSTRICH CLUB.




A STAMP ON THE HAND THAT MEANS THAT THEY HAVE BEEN VACCINATED AND THAT THEY HAVE WELL RESERVED FOR THE EVENING. THIS WILL ALLOW THEM TO GET OUT TO SMOKE FOR EXAMPLE AND RETURN IN THE SAME EVENING.
