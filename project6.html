INDEPENDENT & INFLUENTIAL


PROJECT

I CREATED A PROMOTIONAL POSTER & BROCHURE FOR A FICTIONAL UPCOMING EXHIBITION FEATURING THE WORK OF ALAN FLETCHER.


CHALLENGE

MY PIECES SHOULD EMULATE OR PAY HOMAGE TO THE STYLE OF ALAN FLETCHER, REFLECTED IN THE LAYOUT, FONTS, COLOURS AND COMPOSITION.


SOLUTION

BASED ON THE STYLE OF ALAN FLETCHER I CREATED A NEAT AND MINIMALIST POSTER AND BROCHURE AS HIS STYLE REQUIRES, CAREFULLY RECREATING THE STYLE OF HIS WRITING AND ILLUSTRATIONS WHICH ARE MEANT TO BE MODERN, ELEGANT AND COLORFUL.
