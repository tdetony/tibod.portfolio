PROJECT

CREATED THE WHOLE VISUAL IDENTITY AND WIREFRAME FOR A LAUNDROMAT CONCEPT. THE LAUNDROMAT SHOULD BE A COMFORTABLE PLACE FOR THE CUSTOMERS WHILE THEIR WASHING GOES ON.


CHALLENGE

CREATE A MODERN AND PROFESSIONAL VISUAL IDENTITY AND WIREFRAME FOR A WEBSITE.


SOLUTION

BASED ON A PLAYFUL, CALM, FRIENDLY ATMOSPHERE, I MADE THE WEBSITE/BRAND LOOK EASY TO ACCESS BY CHOOSING SWEET COLORS AND REALLY FRIENDLY TYPOGRAPHY (MOCHE, THE ONE USED FOR THE WASHWASH LOGO).
I ADDED A DRINK MOCKUP AND I IMAGINED HAVING SOFAS, TVS AND WIFI IN THE LAUNDROMAT FOR THE COMFY ASPECT OF THIS ONE. THE CUSTOMERS WILL ALSO BE ABLE TO PAY WITH WASH-BUCKS (WASHWASH CURRENCY). WHEN PEOPLE USE WASH-BUCKS, THEY CAN HAVE REDUCTIONS FOR THEIR WASHING.
